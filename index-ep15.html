Internet of Things:

Definition:

The Internet of Things (IoT) has revolutionized the way we interact with technology, as it allows us to connect everyday devices to the internet and access information and services remotely. To facilitate this, there are many different IoT platforms available, each with their own specialized APIs that cater to specific needs.

Computer Hardware:

Definition:

Computer hardware components are typically classified into two categories: input/output (I/O) devices and internal components.

The internal components of a computer are typically housed inside the computer's case and include the CPU, memory, motherboard, power supply, and storage devices such as hard drives and solid-state drives.

Input/output devices are used to interact with the computer and include the keyboard, mouse, display screen, and speakers.
